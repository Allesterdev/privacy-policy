Privacy Policy - IMC Práctico

Region: United States
Language: English
Last updated: 2025-09-21

1. Information We Collect

The IMC Práctico app may collect the following information:

  • User input data (weight, height, age) for BMI calculations
  • App usage information for analytics and improvements
  • Device identifiers for personalized advertising (through AdMob)
  • App diagnostics and performance data
  • Approximate location information (based on IP address)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Calculate and display your Body Mass Index (BMI)
  • Improve app functionality and performance
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  • Analyze usage patterns for future improvements
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

3. Sharing Information with Third Parties

We may share information with:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying personalized ads
  • Google Analytics: For app usage analytics
  • Service providers: Who help us operate the app
  • Legal authorities: When required by law

4. Your Privacy Choices

You can:

  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings
  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal limitations)
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

5. Children's Information

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete such information immediately.

6. California Privacy (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt out of the sale of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by applicable law.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page.
